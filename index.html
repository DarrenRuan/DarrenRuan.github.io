Research

Vehicles Detection, Tracking and Counting

Kangrui Ruan, Zhen (Sean) Qian, H. Scott Matthews, Chris Hendrickson

In this research, we focus on the vehicles detection, tracking and counting on Forbes Avenue. The detector would output the locations of the bounding boxed and the type of each vehicle, which them would be given into the tracker. When the bounding box passes the counter, it would be counted. By counting the total number of vehicles, we could tell the difference among different periods (temporal) or different locations (spatial). [github]

Scalable Urban Traffic Control by Reinforcement Learning

Kangrui Ruan, Hsu-Chieh Hu, Stephen Smith

First, I have Simulated the practical traffic flow on VISSIM server (SURTRAC) and generated a phase schedule to minimize the cumulative delay of all clusters by a forward-recursion dynamic programming search. Second, I have Applied Policy Iteration Methods to find the best policy for different states situations. Third, by comparing the cumulative delay with the benchmark and the results with traditional methods, I have developed a decentralized control method
